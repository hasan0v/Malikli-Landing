Warranty, Exchanges, and Returns

Return Policy Overview

You can return products of proper quality if their appearance, factory tags, labels, and consumer properties are preserved, and you have a document confirming the purchase. Products that have been used cannot be returned.

According to Article 28 of the Law of the Republic of Belarus dated January 9, 2002, "On Consumer Protection" (hereinafter referred to as the Law), which regulates the return and exchange of products of proper quality, the consumer has the right to return a product of proper quality or exchange it for a similar product within fourteen days.

Return Conditions

For a return to be accepted, the following conditions must be met:

  • The product must be in its original condition with all tags and labels intact
  • Original packaging must be undamaged and complete
  • You must provide proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation)
  • The product must not show any signs of use or wear
  • The return must be initiated within 14 days of receiving the order

How to Return

To initiate a return or exchange, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team via email or phone within the 14-day return period
  2. Provide your order number and details about the items you wish to return
  3. Our team will provide you with a return authorization and instructions
  4. Package the items securely in their original packaging
  5. Send the package to the address provided by our customer service team

Once we receive and inspect the returned items, we will process your refund or exchange within 5-7 business days.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item for a different size or color, please follow the same procedure as for returns. Note that exchanges are subject to availability. If the desired replacement is not available, we will issue a refund for the returned item.

Refunds

Refunds will be processed using the original payment method. Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to appear in your account after we have processed your return. Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Defective Items

If you receive a defective item, please contact our customer service team immediately. For defective products, we offer:

  • Full refund including shipping costs
  • Exchange for the same item
  • Store credit

Please provide photos of the defective item when contacting our customer service team to help us resolve the issue promptly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have to return an item?

You have 14 days from the date of receiving your order to initiate a return or exchange.

Can I return a product if I've worn it or removed the tags?

No, products must be in their original, unused condition with all tags and labels intact to be eligible for return or exchange.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

Yes, customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a mistake on our part or a defective product.

How long will it take to process my refund?

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. It may take an additional few days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider.

What if the item I want to exchange is out of stock?

If the item you want to exchange for is not available, we will issue a refund for the returned item or offer you an alternative product of equal value.

Can I return limited edition or sale items?

Yes, limited edition and sale items follow the same return policy as regular items, provided they meet all the conditions for returns (unused, tags intact, within 14 days).
