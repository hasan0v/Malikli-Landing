Information about available payment options for your orders

Available Payment Methods

Credit & Debit Cards

You can pay for your order using bank cards from the following payment systems:

Visa

All Visa debit and credit cards are accepted

Mastercard

All Mastercard debit and credit cards are accepted

BELKART

Belarusian national payment system cards are accepted

Payment Security

All payments are processed securely using SSL encryption. Your card details are never stored on our servers, ensuring maximum security for your financial information.

When Will I Be Charged?

Your card will be charged immediately upon completing your purchase. The charge will appear on your statement as "MALIKLI1992".

Other Payment Methods

Currently, other payment methods are not available. If you have questions or special requests regarding payment, please contact our customer support team.

Note: All prices are displayed in Belarusian Rubles (BYN). International transactions may incur currency conversion fees from your bank.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

Yes, all payments on our website are processed using secure SSL encryption. Your card details are protected and never stored on our servers.

Do you accept international credit cards?

Yes, we accept Visa and Mastercard issued by banks worldwide. Please note that international transactions may be subject to currency conversion fees from your bank.

When will my card be charged?

Your card will be charged immediately when you complete your purchase. The transaction will appear as "MALIKLI1992" on your statement.

Can I pay with PayPal or other digital wallets?

Currently, we only accept payment via Visa, Mastercard, and BELKART. We are working on adding more payment methods in the future.

What should I do if my payment is declined?

If your payment is declined, please verify that your card details are entered correctly. If the issue persists, contact your bank to ensure there are no restrictions on your card for online purchases. You can also reach out to our customer support team for assistance.

Do you offer invoice payment for businesses?

For business or bulk orders, please contact our customer support team to discuss invoice payment options and corporate purchasing arrangements.
