Shipping FAQ

How is shipping cost calculated?

Shipping costs are calculated based on the number of products and destination country. Each product weighs approximately 400g. We offer transparent pricing: 1 product ships for the base rate, 2-3 products at the 1kg rate, with additional charges for larger orders.

Do you provide tracking information?

Yes, all shipments include full tracking from dispatch to delivery. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order is shipped, allowing you to monitor your package's progress.

What are the delivery timeframes?

Delivery times vary by destination: European Union (7-14 days), Americas (10-21 days), Asia Pacific (7-18 days), and Middle East (7-14 days). Times may vary during peak seasons or due to customs processing.

Are there any customs duties or taxes?

Customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations and the value of your order. These fees are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping costs.

What if my package is damaged or lost?

All packages are insured. If your order arrives damaged or goes missing, please contact us within 48 hours with photos (for damaged items) or tracking information. We'll file a claim and arrange a replacement or refund.

Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

Address changes are possible within 24 hours of placing your order, before it's dispatched. Please contact our customer service immediately with your order number and new address details.
