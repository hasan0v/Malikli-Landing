Information about delivery options and timeframes

Delivery Terms and Timeframes for Belarus

Delivery Methods

We offer several delivery options throughout the Republic of Belarus:

  • Standard Postal Delivery (Belpost) - Available nationwide with an average delivery time of 2-5 business days. The shipping cost is calculated according to Belpost tariffs based on weight and destination.
  • Express Delivery - Available in major cities with delivery within 1-2 business days. Higher shipping rates apply for this premium service.
  • Store Pickup - Available in Minsk at our location on Vera Khoruzhey St., 6A. Orders are typically ready for pickup within 24 hours after confirmation.

Delivery Timeframes

Please note the following estimated delivery timeframes:

  • Minsk: 1-3 business days
  • Regional Centers: 2-4 business days
  • Other Locations: 3-5 business days

These timeframes are estimates and may vary depending on weather conditions, holidays, and other factors.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the following factors:

  • Delivery method selected
  • Package weight and dimensions
  • Delivery destination

Free shipping is available for orders over 100 BYN within Belarus.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can use this tracking number on the Belpost website or contact our customer service team for assistance with tracking your package.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery typically takes 2-5 business days within Belarus. Express delivery options are available in major cities with delivery within 1-2 business days.

Can I change the delivery address after placing an order?

Yes, you can change the delivery address within 24 hours of placing your order. Please contact our customer support team immediately with your order number and the new delivery address. After the 24-hour period, we cannot guarantee that the address change will be processed as your order may have already been prepared for shipping.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on the Belpost website or contact our customer service team for assistance with tracking your package.

What should I do if my package is lost or damaged?

If your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact our customer support team within 48 hours of the expected delivery date. Provide your order number and details of the issue, and we will promptly investigate and resolve the issue with the carrier.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only offer shipping within Belarus. We are working to expand our shipping options to include international destinations in the near future. Please subscribe to our newsletter to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

Is there a minimum order value for free shipping?

Yes, we offer free shipping on all orders over 100 BYN within Belarus. Orders below this amount will have shipping costs calculated based on weight, dimensions, and delivery destination.

What happens if I'm not available to receive my delivery?

If you're not available to receive your delivery, the courier will leave a notification card with instructions on how to collect your package or arrange redelivery. Most packages can be held at the local post office for up to 14 days before being returned to us.
